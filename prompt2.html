Essential Skills to Develop

  1. Understanding AI Fundamentals: Familiarize yourself with artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and natural language processing (NLP). Practical Techniques

  • Gamification: Use gamified learning experiences to practice prompt engineering. This can make the learning process more engaging and help you master various techniques through fun challenges[2].
